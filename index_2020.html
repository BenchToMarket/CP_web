Heart Health .....

Reach Your Cardiac Peak


Our exercise programs are designed by cardiologists specific for cardiac rehabilitation. Our exercises focus to improve efficiency of each heartbeat (delivering more oxygen per beat), heart responsiveness (how fast your HR recovers after exercise & its beat variability / HRV), resting heart rate and more.

This may sound technical, but achieving these goals makes your heart stronger and more efficient. A more efficient heart means it works less for a given amount of physical exertion, ultimately producing less strain on your heart throughout your entire day.


  • 12-week goals:
  • HR 10% lower for all physical exertion
  • 5% more oxygen delivered / heartbeat
  • 5% improvement in heart responsiveness
  • Resting HR lower by 2 BPM (beats/min)
