A Note of Caution

Everyone, including the entrants to the Promotion, should consult your physician or other health care professional before starting this or any other exercise program. This is particularly true if you or your family have a history of high blood pressure or heart disease, or if you have ever experienced chest pain when exercising or have experienced chest pain in the past month when not engaged in physical activity, smoke, have high cholesterol, are obese, or have a bone or joint problem that could be made worse by a change in physical activity. Do not start this exercise program if your physician or health care provider advises against it. If you experience faintness, dizziness, pain or shortness of breath at any time while exercising you should stop immediately.

This fitness app offers exercise information and is designed for educational purposes only. You should not rely on this information as a substitute for, nor does it replace, professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. The use of any information provided on this fitness app is solely at your own risk.

1. Promotion Structure

The Promotion will consist of one Sweepstake. By participating in the “Cardiac Peak Running” (“Sweepstake”), you agree to be bound by these Promotion Official Rules. The Promotion will begin on April 22, 2021 and end on December 31, 2021 (“Promotion Period”), with the final date to enter the promotion November 5, 2021 (“Final Entry Date”). Multiple registrations and entries are permitted as outlined below. Please read these Promotion Official Rules before entering the Promotion. Failure to comply with these Promotion Official Rules may (in the sole discretion of Cardiac Peak) result in disqualification from the Promotion. You must meet the eligibility requirements and follow the Promotion Official Rules regarding participation as set forth here. Cardiac Peak reserves the right to cancel, modify or suspend the Promotion at any time in its sole discretion. The Sweepstake is focused on the Exercise (“Exercise”) of “Running”.

The Promotion will consist of multiple Exercise running challenges (each, a “Challenge”) to accommodate participant’s various exercise preferences and fitness levels, including Challenges for beginners, intermediate and advanced fitness levels. Challenges can be added, altered or inactivated throughout the Promotion Period. Adding, altering or inactivating any Challenge does not affect any terms within these rules, including registration, eligibility, entry, prize or odds of winning. Altering or inactivating a challenge may be to improve the overall exercise and fitness experience and does not affect any previous Entries; all eligible Entry(ies) will remain valid. New Challenges may be introduced to provide more options of exercise, fitness and Entry for each participant.

2. Promotion Registration

To participate in the Promotion, you must first become a registered user of Cardiac Peak (“App”) and provide a valid email address. Cardiac Peak is free to use and there is no purchase necessary. Registration must be completed by the Final Entry Date. The participant (“Participant”) must then select in the App a Running Challenge by searching and then clicking on a Running Challenge in the App, then clicking “Sign up” in the Challenge.

You may participate in each active Challenge during the Promotion Period on or before the Final Entry Date. Multiple Challenges are allowed and you can participate in each Challenge multiple times; however you may not participate in more than one “Running” Challenge, of this Promotion or another Cardiac Peak promotion, at the same time.

Upon “Sign up” for a Running Challenge, the App will create an Exercise Schedule within the App consisting of multiple exercises (each, an Exercise), including an Exercise detail page and schedule date of each Exercise. The Exercise Schedule will be eight (8) weeks in length and considered the entry period (“Entry Period”), with the Beginning Date on the day of registration or the day following registration and the Ending Date eight (8) weeks following the Beginning Date.

Entry Period can not conflict with another Entry Period, of this Promotion or a Sweepstake or Contest of another Cardiac Peak promotion, where the Sweepstake or Contest Exercise is the same. For example, Couch to Cardiac Fit and Cardio Fitness & Weight Loss are both Challenges focused on Running. You may participate in Couch to Cardiac Fit, then only after the Entry Period of Couch to Cardiac Fit has ended and if on or before the Final Entry Date, you may register to participate in Cardio Fitness & Weight Loss (or another Running Challenge of this or another Cardiac Peak Promotion); however, you may participate in Couch to Cardiac Fit and 10,000 Steps concurrently, since 10,000 Steps is a Sweepstake from another Cardiac Peak promotion that is focused on Steps, not Running.

3. Eligibility and Limits

Open to only legal residents of the United States including its commonwealths, territories, and possessions, who are at least 18 years old and meeting the eligibility criteria set forth in these rules. VOID WHERE PROHIBITED OR RESTRICTED BY LAW.

4. How to Enter

Once registered, there are multiple methods of entry (“Entry”): with a Select Fitness Tracker and two (2) Alternative Method of Entry, as described below. Limit one (1) Entry per day per participant.

To enter with a Select Fitness Tracker - Participant (“Participant”) will automatically receive one (1) entry (“Entry”) for every exercise started during the Entry Period with a Select Fitness Tracker and that exercise successfully communicated to the App. Neither completion nor adherence of the Exercise are required for Entry, only that the Exercise is started and at least one (1) second of Exercise is communicated to App by a Select Fitness Tracker. Multiple Entry(ies) are allowed, but limited to one (1) Entry per day per Sweepstake.

Alternative Method of Entry with App: To enter without a Select Fitness Tracker by Manual Submission - Participant will receive one (1) Entry during the Entry Period when a participant records an Exercise by Manual Submission in the App. Manual Submission must be submitted on the day the exercise is scheduled, but Participant may submit an Entry on every day an exercise is scheduled for the Sweepstake. The Participant does not need to start or complete the Exercise in order for Entry by Manual Submission. Multiple Entry(ies) are allowed, but limited to one (1) Entry per day per Sweepstake.

Alternative Method of Entry without the App: Participant will receive one (1) Entry during the Promotion Period when participant posts on Cardiac Peak’s facebook page at https://www.facebook.com/CardiacPeak or sends an email to enter.running@cardiacpeak.com. Post or email must include a description and/or image of that day’s exercise. Each entry must be unique to that day. You must follow all rules of facebook with any post, including but not limited to copyright laws. Multiple Entry(ies) are allowed, but limited to one (1) Entry per day per Sweepstake.

5. Prize

One Prize for the Sweepstake. The Prize is a Gift Card with a value of $100 USD. Payment of prize to be made by Cardiac Peak. Since each participant can enter multiple times, the odds of winning the prize in the Sweepstake will depend on the total number eligible Entry(ies) of the Sweepstake during the Promotion Period from all participants and the number of your Entry(ies) of the Sweepstake. For example, if you have ten (10) Entry(ies) and the total number of Entry(ies) from all participants are one thousand (1,000), your odds of winning would be 10 in 1,000.

6. Selection of Winner

For the Promotion Period, the prize will be awarded in an individual random drawing among all eligible Entry(ies) received for the Sweepstake during the entire Promotion Period. If you registered for a Sweepstake multiple times during the Promotion Period, all eligible Entry(ies) from the registered Sweepstake will be included in the random drawing.

7. Winner Notification

The potential winner will be notified by email within fourteen (14) days from the end of Promotion to the email provided during registration of the App or on file with Cardiac Peak, if email was updated or emailed to Cardiac Peak. The participant is responsible for providing an accurate email. The potential winner will be required to provide an address in which to send the prize within fourteen (14) days the winning email is sent to the winner. This address will not be used for any other purposes. If the potential winner entered by facebook post, Cardiac Peak will send a message through facebook, requesting the potential winners address in which to send the prize (same timelines and deadlines as above).

In the event the potential winner is deemed ineligible and/or does not claim their prize by dates specified herein, an alternate potential winner may be selected in accordance with these Official Rules. One attempt to contact any potential winner will be conducted. If attempt fails, the prize will be forfeited, and the prize will not be awarded to the potential winner.
